`cinematic storyboard frame, <shot type>, <subject>, <action>, exact location: <location>, time and atmosphere: <time>, emotion: <emotion>, camera: <camera>, lighting: <lighting>, lens/look: <lens>, background details from script: <details>, continuity constraints: stable identity, consistent outfit, no text, no watermark`
