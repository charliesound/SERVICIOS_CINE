# Script Notes - LA NOCHE ETERNAL
## Feature Film - Night Exterior Sequence

### Scene 1: EXT. ALAMEDA PARK - NIGHT
**Location**: Alameda Park, Madrid
**Time**: Night
**Description**: Main character MARIA walks through the park alone.

**SH01**: Wide establishing shot. Maria enters frame from left, walks across park, exits right.
- Lighting: Moonlight, practical street lamps
- Mood: Melancholic, mysterious
- Notes: establish the park location

**SH02**: Medium shot. Maria walking, looking around nervously.
- Focus: her reaction to sounds
- Notes: build tension

**SH03**: Close-up. Maria's face, stops, looks at something in the bushes.
- Notes: key reaction moment

### Scene 2: INT. PARK BENCH - NIGHT
**Time**: Moments later
**Description**: Maria sits on a bench, takes out a letter.

**SH01**: Wide shot. Maria sits on bench, unfolds letter.
- Notes: intimate moment

**SH02**: Over-the-shoulder. Letter in hands, reading.
- Notes: emotion beats

**SH03**: Close-up. Maria's emotional reaction - tears.
- Notes: payoff moment

## Technical Notes

### Shooting Days
- Day 1: Scene 1 (EXT Alameda Park)
- Day 2: Scene 2 (INT bench)

### Camera
- ARRI Alexa Mini LF
- Lenses: Zeiss Supreme Prime 35mm, 50mm, 85mm
- Format: ARRIRAW 4.6K
- Color: ARRI LogC4

### Sound
- Mixer: Sound Devices 888
- Mics: Senn MKH 416 (boom), DPA 6060 (lav)
- Sample Rate: 48kHz
- Bit Depth: 24

### Circle Notes
- Scene 1: SH01 TK01 and TK02 both circled
- Scene 2: SH01 TK01 circled

## Director's Intentions

1. Create atmosphere of unease
2. Focus on Maria's emotional state
3. Build to emotional payoff in SH03 of Scene 2
4. Use natural night lighting
5. Emphasize isolation
