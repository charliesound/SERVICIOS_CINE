# Camera Motion Dictionary

- Zoom in: subtle emphasis on realization or threat.
- Zoom out: reveal space, isolation, or vulnerability.
- Pan left: discover information toward the left side of frame.
- Pan right: discover information toward the right side of frame.
- Tilt up: reveal height, danger, or awe.
- Tilt down: reveal detail, evidence, or fragility.
- Dolly in: push toward emotional intensity.
- Dolly out: detach or reveal environmental context.
- Orbital arc: circle subject when dramatic tension shifts.
- Pull back: widen distance after impact or revelation.
- Handheld subtle: restrained instability for suspense or proximity.

## Rule

- Use one or two camera moves maximum per shot prompt.
