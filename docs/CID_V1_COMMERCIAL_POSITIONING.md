# AILinkCinema + CID - Commercial Positioning

## Core Positioning

AILinkCinema is an agency specialized in detecting real bottlenecks inside the audiovisual industry and solving them through applied artificial intelligence for production, distribution, post-production, document management and operational workflows.

CID - Cine Inteligente Digital - is one of its main applications: a platform designed so producers, production managers, directors, assistant directors, editors, distributors and cinemas can work in harmony inside the same audiovisual project.

## Brand Distinction

### AILinkCinema
- Agency / consultancy
- Diagnoses workflow problems
- Designs AI solutions around real production needs
- Can propose CID, document agents, automation flows, integrations or custom tooling

### CID
- Concrete application inside AILinkCinema
- Coordinates audiovisual projects from script to sales and post-production
- Keeps teams aligned around shared information
- Reduces duplication, disorder and communication loss

## What CID Solves Today

- Script versioning and impact visibility
- AI-assisted storyboard support
- Estimated budgeting
- Funding opportunity organization
- Producer dossier generation
- Distribution and commercial pack preparation
- Basic commercial CRM
- Change approval and shotlist support
- Media scan, reports and editorial preparation
- DaVinci-oriented premontage and FCPXML export

## Who It Is For

- Production companies
- Executive producers
- Production management teams
- Directors and assistant directors
- Editors and post-production teams
- Distribution and sales teams

## Honest Limits

- Budget outputs are estimates and require production review
- Funding opportunities must be verified with official sources
- CID does not guarantee distribution or platform acceptance
- Editorial output requires validation in the client environment
- The platform supports professionals; it does not replace them
