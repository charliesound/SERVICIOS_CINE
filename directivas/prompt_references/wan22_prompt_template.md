# Wan2.2 Prompt Template

## Template

- 1 subject
- 1 action
- 1 location
- 1 time

## Positive Prompt Template

`cinematic storyboard frame, <shot type>, <subject>, <action>, exact location: <location>, time and atmosphere: <time>, emotional tone: <emotion>, camera: <camera>, lighting: <lighting>, lens/look: <lens>, background details from script: <details>, continuity constraints: stable identity, consistent outfit, prop continuity, no extra elements`

## Constraints

- stable identity
- consistent outfit
- no text
- no watermark
- no extra characters
- no props outside the script
